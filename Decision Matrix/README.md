The Story: Decisions are hard when you have too many variables. This tool brings logic to the chaos by using math to weigh what actually matters.

The Problem: Choosing between career paths or complex business options often involves subjective feelings rather than objective data.

The Solution: I built a Weighted Scoring Model that allows a user to assign an "Importance Factor" (weight) to different criteria (e.g., Pay vs. Enjoyment vs. Market demand) to reach a logical choice based on the user's personal priorities.

Key Skills:

Data Modeling & Quantitative Analysis.

Applying weighted averages to normalize subjective decision-making.

Creating a reusable template for multi-criteria decision analysis (MCDA).
